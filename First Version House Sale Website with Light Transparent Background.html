Stunning 5-Bedroom House for Sale in Pyrgos

Description

This 250 m² house, built in 2010, includes an antique hall and is located on a 600 m² plot in the center of Pyrgos, a suburb of Limassol. This area has a rich history, being the birthplace of perfumery thanks to Queen Cleopatra.

The house features 5 bedrooms, a separate kitchen, a veranda, and a swimming pool. There are also 3 bathrooms, a separate shower by the pool, and plenty of utility rooms, allowing the house to be transformed into a mini-hotel.

Advantages include the antique hall, one-meter thick stone walls providing unique thermal insulation, a foyer, and a separate kitchen. The house is equipped with underfloor heating, air conditioning, a separate laundry room, and one of the five bedrooms overlooking the pool. There is also potential for solar panels on the flat roof.

Price: €900,000 (negotiable)
